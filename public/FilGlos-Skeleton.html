Word Detail

Gukom
noun Subanen · Kolibugan
Translations
🇵🇭
Filipino
Pinuno ng tribu
🇬🇧
English
Tribal elder / leader
Definition

A Gukom is a respected elder or recognized leader within the Subanen or Kolibugan community. The Gukom holds authority in matters of customary law, land governance, rituals, and conflict resolution. Their word carries deep cultural and spiritual weight.

Example sentence

"Ang Gukom ay nagtipon upang pag-usapan ang karapatan sa lupa ng tribu."

The Gukom gathered to discuss the tribe's land rights.

Cultural context

In Subanen society, the Gukom system is the backbone of indigenous governance. Decisions about communal resources, marriage, and rituals are made collectively by the Gukom council. Disrespecting a Gukom is considered a serious cultural offense. This role has been validated and documented with the blessing of community elders in Zamboanga Sibugay.

Etymology
Proto-Austronesian root

Derived from the root word ukom, meaning "to judge" or "to govern." The prefix gu- elevates it to a title of authority — one who governs.
