Definition

A {{WORD}} is a respected elder or recognized leader within the Subanen or Kolibugan community. The {{WORD}} holds authority in matters of customary law, land governance, rituals, and conflict resolution. Their word carries deep cultural and spiritual weight.

"Ang {{WORD}} ay nagtipon upang pag-usapan ang karapatan sa lupa ng tribu."

Cultural context

In Subanen society, the {{WORD}} system is the backbone of indigenous governance. Decisions about communal resources, marriage, and rituals are made collectively by the Gukom council. Disrespecting a Gukom is considered a serious cultural offense. This role has been validated and documented with the blessing of community elders in Zamboanga Sibugay.

Etymology
Proto-Austronesian root

Derived from the root word ukom, meaning "to judge" or "to govern." The prefix gu- elevates it to a title of authority — one who governs.
