\## 📋 Project SIBUGAY — Summary



\*\*Full Title:\*\* \*Pagpapayaman ng Pamanang Kultural ng Subanen/Kolibugan sa pamamagitan ng Interaktibong Digital Glosaryo ng Subanen/Kolibugan–Filipino–Ingles para sa Edukasyong IP-ED sa Zamboanga Sibugay\*



\*\*In English:\*\* Enriching the Cultural Heritage of the Subanen/Kolibugan through an Interactive Digital Glossary (Subanen/Kolibugan–Filipino–English) for IP-ED Schools in Zamboanga Sibugay



\*\*Institution:\*\* Western Mindanao State University – Ipil External Campus

\*\*Researchers:\*\* Jess Kevin P. Pasgala \& Rani A. Ruing

\*\*Duration:\*\* 12 months | \*\*Budget:\*\* ₱105,000



\*\*The Problem it Solves:\*\* The indigenous languages of the Subanen and Kolibugan peoples — especially their "deep words" tied to beliefs, culture, and nature — are slowly dying out. There are no modern, accessible digital tools to preserve and teach them, especially for IP-ED (Indigenous Peoples Education) schools.



\*\*The Solution:\*\* Build an interactive digital glossary app with at least 300 documented indigenous terms, validated by Gukom (Tribal Elders), with translations in Filipino and English.

\## 📱 App Features / Modes



Based on the proposal, here's what the app needs to do:



\### 1. 📖 Glossary / Dictionary Mode (Core Feature)

\- Browse or search \*\*≥300 Subanen/Kolibugan words\*\*

\- Each entry shows:

&#x20; - The indigenous word

&#x20; - \*\*Filipino translation\*\*

&#x20; - \*\*English translation\*\*

&#x20; - \*\*Definition / meaning\*\*

&#x20; - \*\*Example sentence\*\* in context

&#x20; - \*\*Cultural explanation\*\* (background/significance of the word)

&#x20; - \*\*Etymology / historical origin\*\* (where applicable)



\### 2. 🔊 Audio Pronunciation Feature

\- Each word has a \*\*recorded audio clip\*\* from a native speaker

\- User taps a button to hear the correct pronunciation

\- Critical for a dying language — written text alone isn't enough



\### 3. 🎮 Interactive Mini-Activities

\- The proposal explicitly mentions \*\*"mini-activities"\*\* as a feature

\- These are likely vocabulary quizzes, matching games, or fill-in-the-blank exercises to reinforce learning

\- Designed for students in IP-ED schools



\### 4. 🏫 Learning / Educational Mode

\- Targeted at \*\*teachers and students\*\*

\- Integrated with IP-ED curriculum

\- Should function as a \*\*digital learning resource\*\* usable in classrooms

\- Training materials/modules were mentioned as companion outputs



\### 5. 🌐 Trilingual Interface

\- The entire glossary operates in \*\*three languages simultaneously\*\*: Subanen/Kolibugan ↔ Filipino ↔ English

\- Users should be able to look up a word from any of the three languages



\### 6. 🔍 Search \& Browse

\- Search bar for quick lookup

\- Browse by alphabetical order or category (cultural context, rituals, nature, beliefs, etc.)



\### 7. 🗂️ Cultural Context / Archive Mode

\- Each word can have extended \*\*cultural notes\*\* — connecting language to rituals, oral traditions, and community practices

\- This makes it not just a dictionary but a \*\*living cultural archive\*\*
